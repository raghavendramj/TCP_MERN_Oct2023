Applying Colors - Different Ways

Color
Hexadecimal
RGB - Red Blue Green
rgba - Red Blue Green + Lightness
HSL - Hue Saturation and Lightness
HSLA - Hue Saturation and Lightness + A

HSL stands for Hue, Saturation, and Lightness.

  • Saturation can be described as the intensity of a color.
  • It is a percentage value from 0% to 100%.

  • The lightness of a color can be described as how much light you want to give the color.
  • 0% means no light (dark)
  • 50% means 50% light (neither dark nor light)
  • and 100% means full light.
